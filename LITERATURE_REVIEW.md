## 二、科学表述准确性核查

### about.html

| 表述 | 准确性 | 修正建议 |

| "灰蝶科全球约6000种" | ✅ 基本准确 | 实际5000-7000种，可写"约6000种" |
| "约75%的物种在生活史某个阶段与蚂蚁发生互作" | ⚠️ **偏高** | Pierce(2002)原文：~30%有专性互作，~75%有某种形式关联。需加限定词："有某种形式的关联" |

## 三、需要补充的关键文献

### 缺失的核心文献（应添加）

1. **Thomas JA & Settele J (2004). Butterfly declines — perspectives from the UK and Europe. *Oecologia* 139:269-275.**
   - 大蓝蝶保护经典论文，补充lifecycle页面

2. **DeVries PJ (1990). Enhancement of symbioses between lycaenids and ants by vibrational communication. *Science* 248:1104-1106.**
   - 替代Seymour 2023！灰蝶科声学通信的开创性论文

3. **Akino T et al. (1999). Chemical mimicry in the wolf spider *Pardosa milvina*. *Physiol Entomol* 24:354-362.**
   - CHC化学拟态经典案例（虽然不是蝴蝶，但原理相同）

4. **Hojo MK et al. (2009). Chemical disguise as particular cases of signal mimicry. *J Exp Biol* 212:3385-3395.**
   - CHC化学拟态综述，补充chemical.html

5. **Nash DR et al. (2008). Surface cuticular hydrocarbons of queen ants mediate acceptance of nestmate workers by the parasite *Maculinea rebeli*. *Proc R Soc B* 275:3511-3518.**
   - Maculinea CHC拟态直接证据！

6. **Dani FR et al. (2005). Can cuticular hydrocarbons provide species-specific cues? *J Chem Ecol* 31:1699-1714.**
   - CHC作为物种识别信号

7. **Schönrogge K et al. (2004). The biology of the social parasite *Maculinea arion* (Lepidoptera: Lycaenidae). *Ann Entomol Soc Am* 97:219-231.**
   - 替代可疑的Schönrogge 1999引用

8. **Fiedler K (2001). Ants-Lycaenid interactions: recent findings and new directions. *Entomol Sci* 4:183-188.**
   - 补充mutualism页面的现代综述
